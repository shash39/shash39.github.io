About Sprimet Auto Forms

Founded in 1961, Sprimet Auto Forms has been engaged in the manufacturing of springs from round and flat wire for the automotive industry for over 60 years.

We specialize in products catering to critical automotive brake components and life-saving systems. Our focus is on incorporating the latest technologies to manufacture 100% machine-made goods, constantly upgrading our systems and training our staff for anticipated future demands.
