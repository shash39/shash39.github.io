About Us

Sprimet Auto Forms has been a pioneer in the manufacture of springs from round and flat wire since 1961, primarily for the automotive sector.

We prioritize machine precision, efficient lead times, and the ability to deliver complex geometries with consistent quality, serving life-critical components in braking and engine systems.
