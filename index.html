Legacy & Evolution

Tencom Industrial Corporation was founded in the late 1950s when Shri Vidya Prakash set up a manufacturing venture, becoming one of the first to import German and Soviet technology into the country and establish a spring manufacturing unit in the subcontinent.

Today, we operate as Sprimet Springs, specializing in the design, development, and manufacturing of high-performance springs, including:

  • Tension Springs
  • Compression Springs
  • Torsion Springs
  • Wire Forms
  • Strip Springs
